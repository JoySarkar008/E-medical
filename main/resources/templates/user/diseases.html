Respiratory Problems

Symptoms:

Coughing, shortness of breath, wheezing, chest pain or tightness.
Specialists: Pulmonologist (lung specialist).

Cardiovascular Problems

Symptoms:

Chest pain, shortness of breath, palpitations, dizziness, fainting.
Specialists: Cardiologist (heart specialist).

Gastrointestinal Problems

Symptoms:

Abdominal pain, bloating, diarrhea, constipation, heartburn.
Specialists: Gastroenterologist.

Neurological Problems

Symptoms:

Headaches, dizziness, numbness or weakness, seizures, memory loss.
Specialists: Neurologist.

Musculoskeletal Problems

Symptoms:

Joint pain, muscle weakness, stiffness, swelling, limited range of motion.
Specialists: Orthopedist or Rheumatologist.

Dermatological Problems

Symptoms:

Skin rashes, itching, dryness, lesions, discoloration.
Specialists: Dermatologist.

Endocrine Problems

Symptoms:

Fatigue, weight changes, excessive thirst or urination, heat or cold intolerance.
Specialists: Endocrinologist.

Psychological/Behavioral Problems:

Symptoms:

Mood swings, anxiety, depression, sleep disturbances, cognitive changes.
Specialists: Psychiatrist or Psychologist.

Reproductive Health Problems

Symptoms:

Irregular menstruation, pelvic pain, erectile dysfunction, infertility.
Specialists: Gynecologist (for women) or Urologist (for men).

Ophthalmological Problems

Symptoms:

Blurred vision, eye pain, redness, discharge, floaters.
Specialists: Ophthalmologist.
